Basal Body Temperature (BBT) is a body temperature at the wake up time after having a good sleep overnight measuring before any other activities.

It can be used as a tool to estimate the ovulation day

1. Pre-Ovulation period In this period, the level of Estrogen hormone will be higher and higher which cause the body temperature decreasing.

2. Ovulation period In this period, the level of Estrogen hormone will reach the highest of the month. That means the body temperature will be reach the lowest level of the month.

3. Post-Ovulation period In this period, the level of Estrogen hormone will be decreasing while the level of Progesterone hormone will be higher and cause the body temperature higher.

Using this application to plot the BBT chart

Every morning, the body temperature is needed to be measured by a thermometer to make it more accurate since the difference in temperature will be only 0.2-0.3 degree celsius (about 0.5 degree fahrenheit). After record for a few month, the pattern of ovulation will be shown. To make it more accurate, the intercourse should be done 1-2 days before the ovulation date to increase the chance of pregnancy.
